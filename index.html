AI

As many people are, I am fascinated by AI, especially since the inception of GPT-2 in 2019. Experimenting with prompts and other people's pre-trained models is fun for a while, but also pretty limited. I am also fascinated by specialized applications of AI, not only large language models.

That being said, I am by no means an AI expert. As a bird's-eyes-introduction for people like me, I can strongly recommend the book "You look like a thing and I love you" by Janelle Shane.

Like most people in IT, I am deeply fascinated by the possibilities of Deep Learning and other AI technologies.
